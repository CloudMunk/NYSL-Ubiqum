Welcome to NYSL

Our Mission

To support young athletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods,who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, specifically those whose skills around team cooperation and good sportsmanship.

Our Vision

The Northside Youth Soccer League aspires to devlop strong, well-rounded, and mindful athletes through building of character, self-discipline, and leadership.

General Information

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside an environment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
